## Q1 — Pager gesture feel?

Options: Photos-lite snap+morph+dismiss (recommended) / Full
Photos-feel / Instagram hard-snap only.

**Answer: Photos-lite.** Snap paging + tile↔detail morph +
interactive swipe-down; zoom carryover and pinch-to-grid skipped as
a later polish candidate.

## Q2 — AirPlay / mirroring / recording policy?

Options: Block + blank-on-capture (recommended) / Allow, it's my TV /
Preference defaulted blocked.

**Answer: Allow — "it's my TV."** External playback permitted on the
owner's ecosystem; screen recording still blanks the player.
(Recommendation overridden by the user — recorded as deliberate.)

## Q3 — Live Photo motion this leg?

**Answer: redirected into autoplay — videos AUTOPLAY when swiped to,
Instagram style.** Clarified in Q3b: muted autoplay + tap-for-sound,
looping; Live Photos auto-play motion once on landing.

## Q4 — Standalone audio scope?

**Answer: Video-only.** PHPicker cannot import audio; the loader
stays format-agnostic for a future Files-import leg.

**Answer: Defer.** Plain scrubber; seek latency is the benchmark's
